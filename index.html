Debt Repayment Tips

Create a Budget

Track your expenses and identify areas where you can cut back to increase your monthly payments.

Balance Transfer

Consider transferring high-interest debt to a card with 0% introductory APR to save on interest.

Increase Payments

Even small increases in monthly payments can significantly reduce your repayment time.

Stop Using Cards

Pause using credit cards while paying off debt to prevent further accumulation.

Alternative Methods

Debt Consolidation

Combine multiple debts into a single loan with a lower interest rate.

  • Simplifies payments
  • Potentially lower interest
  • Fixed repayment term

Debt Settlement

Negotiate with creditors to pay less than the full amount owed.

  • Reduces total debt
  • Faster resolution
  • Professional assistance
